Welcome

Welcome to Women in Tech, a global initiative aimed at closing the gender gap in the technology industry.

Women in Tech South Africa officially launched in April 2020 with a dual mission: to help close the gender gap in technology and to help more women and girls embrace STEM fields.

We are a registered non-profit organization taking action toward the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), including Education, Innovation, Social Inclusion, and Diversity & Equality in the workplace.
